Place your Android app build here.

The download buttons on the site point to:  downloads/yourapp.apk

To activate downloads:
1. Build your APK (e.g. app-release.apk).
2. Rename it to  yourapp.apk  and drop it in this folder,
   OR keep your own filename and update the href in index.html
   (search for "downloads/yourapp.apk" — there are 4 links).

Note: some static hosts need MIME type application/vnd.android.package-archive
configured for .apk files to download instead of erroring.
