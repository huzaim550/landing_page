Frequently asked questions

Is this legal?

Yes. Manzar only carries content that is in the public domain (its copyright has expired) or distributed free-to-air by the broadcaster. There are no pirated or encrypted premium channels on the service.

What is an "Xtream line"?

It's a set of three details — a server URL, a username, and a password — that you type into an Xtream-compatible IPTV app. The app then pulls the whole live TV and movie lineup automatically.

Which apps and devices work?

Anything that supports Xtream Codes login: IPTV Smarters, TiViMate, GSE Smart IPTV, Perfect Player, Kodi (with an Xtream add-on), and VLC. That covers Android, iOS, Fire TV, Android TV, Windows and Mac.

How do I get access?

Fill in the request to join form with your name, email, and phone number. We review requests and, once you're approved, email your Xtream line (server URL, username and password) so you can start watching right away.

How much does it cost?

Requesting to join is free and there's no checkout on this page. If any charges apply to your line we'll make that clear when we reach out — you're never billed just for sending a request.
